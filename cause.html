About The Cause

New York City being one of the major cities in America, is no exception to havng many homeless people. According to coalition for the homeless , there are now around 57,016 people who are homeless. NYC now has the highest homeless levels since the 1930s. Many homeless peopel also suffer fro mental illness and sleep on the streets or in the subway system. We believe that no person should go without a home and that trains and the streets are no place for good living conditions. While shelters are not perfect, they are a better alternative for people. This website is designed to help homeless people throughout the city find shelters closest to them. Click on the Map tab
